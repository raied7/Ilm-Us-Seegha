Final-Exam Study Guide

Built from ʿIlm uṣ-Ṣīgha — the rules of mahmūz and muʿtall (Lesson 22–29) and how they apply in the tasareef (Lessons 24, 30–42). Everything works offline. Tap a card to flip; swipe tables sideways on mobile.

١ Rules Reference
All 9 mahmūz rules (+ extensions) and 25 muʿtall rules, each with its signature example and derivation. Filterable.
٢ Tasareef Tables
Full 14-ṣīgha conjugations for every category — mahmūz, mithāl, ajwaf, nāqiṣ, lafīf, muḍāʿaf, murakkabāt — with the original → final derivation written out and linked to the rule that caused each change.
٣ Active Recall
Flashcards (rule recall + derivation chains), a matching game (rule ↔ example), and a quiz with MCQs and step-by-step derivation questions — exactly the format your exam uses.
★ How to use this the week before
Mon–Tue: Read Rules, then Tasareef tables — recite each table aloud RTL.
Wed–Thu: Flashcards on "Derivation" mode until you can produce the chain from memory.
Fri: Matching + full Quiz; redo any rule you miss.
Sat AM: Quiz "derivation" questions only — that's the heavy part of the test.
